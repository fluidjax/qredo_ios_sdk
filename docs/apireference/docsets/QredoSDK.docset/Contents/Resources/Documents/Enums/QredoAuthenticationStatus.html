QredoAuthenticationStatus

enum QredoAuthenticationStatus : NSUInteger {}

Represents the authentication status of a Conversation. This will be red, amber or green and is set by calling otherPartyHasMyFingerPrint or iHaveRemoteFingerPrint

  • QREDO_RED

    Neither party has confirmed their Conversationpublic key fingerprint

    Objective-C

    Swift

  • QREDO_AMBER

    Only one party has confirmed their Conversation public key fingerprint

  • QREDO_GREEN

    Both parties have confirmed their Conversation public key fingerprints. The Conversation is fully authenticated
